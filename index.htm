Hello, I'm Chiara Lisotti

PhD Student in Astroparticle Physics

I'm part of the Astroparticle Physics group at the University of Sydney. I work on neutrino and dark matter phenomenology. My work focuses on direct detection of these elusive particles, and in particular on directional detection technologies.
